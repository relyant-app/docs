title: "Quick Setup Checklist"
description: "Everything you need to do to get your Relyant organization fully configured"
---

Use this checklist to track your onboarding progress. Complete each step before moving to the next module.

## Organization setup

- [ ] [Create your organization](/getting-started/create-your-organization)
- [ ] Configure [organization settings](/settings/organization-settings) (name, time zone, logo)
- [ ] [Invite your team members](/getting-started/invite-your-team) and assign roles

## Locations

- [ ] [Add at least one building](/locations/add-a-building)
- [ ] [Add floors and rooms](/locations/add-floors-and-rooms) within each building

## Assets

- [ ] [Set up asset categories](/assets/categories-and-custom-fields)
- [ ] [Add your first asset](/assets/add-an-asset) and assign it to a location

## Maintenance

- [ ] Review [work order settings](/settings/work-order-settings) (priorities, statuses)
- [ ] [Create your first work order](/maintenance/work-orders/create-a-work-order)

## Bookings (if applicable)

- [ ] Review [booking settings](/settings/booking-settings)
- [ ] [Configure booking types](/bookings/location-bookings/manage-booking-types)
- [ ] [Reserve a room](/bookings/location-bookings/reserve-a-room) to verify the setup

## Procurement (if applicable)

- [ ] [Add your first supplier](/procurement/suppliers/add-a-supplier)
- [ ] [Create a purchase request](/procurement/purchase-requests/create-a-purchase-request)

## Service Requests (if applicable)

- [ ] [Configure request types](/service-requests/configure-request-types)
- [ ] [Set up approval workflows](/service-requests/set-up-approval-workflows)

---

Once you've completed the checklist, your organization is ready for day-to-day use. Reach out to [support@relyant.app](mailto:support@relyant.app) if you need help with any step.
