Relyant is a [work order and facilities management platform](https://relyant.app) designed for educational institutions. It brings together asset tracking, maintenance workflows, room and equipment bookings, inventory management, procurement, and service requests into a single system.

## Who is Relyant for?

Relyant is built for operations and facilities teams at schools, colleges, and other educational institutions:

- **IT departments** managing devices, infrastructure, and service requests
- **Facilities managers** handling maintenance, repairs, and preventive care
- **Operations teams** coordinating bookings, procurement, and inventory
- **Administrators** overseeing staff, workflows, and reporting

## What can you do in Relyant?

| Module | What you can do |
|---|---|
| **Assets** | Track equipment, devices, and infrastructure with photos, barcodes, and custom fields |
| **Locations** | Organize your campus into buildings, floors, and rooms |
| **Inventory** | Monitor stock levels and process item requests |
| **Maintenance** | Create work orders, accept work requests, and schedule preventive maintenance |
| **Bookings** | Reserve rooms and assets with scheduling and approval workflows |
| **Procurement** | Manage suppliers, purchase requests, and purchase orders |
| **Service Requests** | Configure request types and manage approval workflows |
| **Members** | Invite team members and assign roles |
| **Reports** | View analytics and export data |
