---
title: "Reserve an Asset"
description: "Book a specific piece of equipment or asset for a time period"
---

## Steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Asset Bookings">
    In the left sidebar, click **Bookings**, then **Asset Bookings**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click New Booking">
    Click the **New Booking** button.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select the asset">
    Search for the asset you want to book. Only assets marked as **bookable** will appear in the list. If an asset isn't available for the time you want, it will show as unavailable.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set the date and time">
    Select the start and end date/time for your booking.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter booking details">
    Fill in:
    - **Purpose** — what you're using the asset for
    - **Quantity** — if the asset has capacity (e.g., a set of laptops), how many units you need
    - **Notes** — any additional context
  </Step>
  <Step title="Submit">
    Click **Book**. If approval is required, the booking will be **Pending**. Otherwise, it's immediately **Confirmed**.
  </Step>
</Steps>
