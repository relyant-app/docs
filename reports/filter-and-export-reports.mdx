---
title: "Filter and Export Reports"
description: "Narrow down data by date, module, or category and export it for external use"
---

<Note>
  **Required role:** **Admin** or **Manager**
</Note>

## Filter report data

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Reports">
    In the left sidebar, click **Reports**, then select the specific report type you want (e.g., Work Orders, Assets, Bookings).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Apply filters">
    Use the filter panel to narrow down data by:
    - **Date range** — start and end date
    - **Status** — e.g., Open, Completed, Pending
    - **Assigned to** — filter by technician or team member
    - **Location** — filter by building, floor, or room
    - **Category** — filter by asset or item category
  </Step>
  <Step title="View the filtered results">
    The report updates in real time as you apply filters. The summary cards and charts reflect the filtered data.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Export report data

<Steps>
  <Step title="Apply filters (optional)">
    Filter the data as described above to export only the records you need.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Export">
    Click the **Export** button in the top-right corner of the report.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose a format">
    Select your preferred export format:
    - **CSV** — for use in spreadsheet tools like Excel or Google Sheets
    - **PDF** — for sharing or printing
  </Step>
  <Step title="Download">
    The file will download to your device. Large exports may take a moment to prepare.
  </Step>
</Steps>
