Reports in Relyant give you visibility into your organization's operations â€” maintenance performance, asset utilization, booking trends, procurement spend, and more. Use the analytics dashboard for at-a-glance insights, or filter and export data for deeper analysis.

## What you can report on

| Module | Metrics available |
|---|---|
| **Work Orders** | Volume, completion rate, average resolution time, by technician |
| **Assets** | Total count, by category, by location, under maintenance |
| **Bookings** | Utilization rate, most booked spaces, booking trends |
| **Inventory** | Stock levels, item requests, fulfilment rate |
| **Procurement** | Purchase request volume, spend by supplier, PO status |
| **Service Requests** | Request volume, by type, average response time |

<Note>
  **Required role:** **Admin** or **Manager** can view and export reports.
</Note>
