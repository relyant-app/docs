description: "Get a high-level overview of your organization's operational performance"
---

<Note>
  **Required role:** **Admin** or **Manager**
</Note>

The analytics dashboard gives you a summary of key metrics across your organization, updated in real time.

## Steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Reports">
    In the left sidebar, click **Reports**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="View the dashboard">
    The dashboard loads automatically with summary cards showing:
    - Open work orders
    - Work orders completed this month
    - Active assets
    - Upcoming bookings
    - Low-stock inventory items
    - Pending purchase requests
  </Step>
  <Step title="Explore charts">
    Scroll down to see charts for:
    - Work order volume over time
    - Work order completion rate
    - Booking utilization by location
    - Asset distribution by category
  </Step>
  <Step title="Switch the date range">
    Use the date picker at the top of the dashboard to view data for the last 7 days, 30 days, 3 months, 12 months, or a custom range.
  </Step>
</Steps>
