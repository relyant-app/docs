---
title: "Submit a Work Request"
description: "Report a maintenance issue or repair need to your facilities team"
---

Any Relyant member can submit a work request to flag a maintenance issue. This is the starting point for getting a repair or maintenance task completed.

## Steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Work Requests">
    In the left sidebar, click **Maintenance**, then **Work Requests**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Submit Request">
    Click the **Submit Request** button.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Describe the issue">
    Fill in the request form:
    - **Title** — a short summary of the issue (e.g., "Leaking tap in Staff Bathroom")
    - **Description** — more detail about the problem, when it started, and any relevant context
    - **Location** — the building, floor, or room where the issue is (optional but recommended)
    - **Asset** — the specific asset involved, if applicable (optional)
    - **Priority suggestion** — indicate how urgent you think the issue is
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add a photo (optional)">
    Attach a photo of the issue to help the maintenance team understand what needs to be done.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Submit">
    Click **Submit**. Your request will be sent to the Admin or Manager for review. You'll receive a notification when the status changes.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Tracking your request

After submitting, you can view the status of your request in **Maintenance → Work Requests → My Requests**. You'll be notified when it's converted to a work order or rejected.
